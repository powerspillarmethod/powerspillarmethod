Why most health approaches fail

Most coaching programs focus on one thing — usually food. But your health is the sum of four interconnected systems. When one is off, all four suffer.

This audit helps you score yourself honestly across all four pillars so you can see the full picture — and know exactly where to start.

"You can't out-eat poor sleep. You can't out-supplement chronic stress. And you can't build lasting wellness in isolation. True health is holistic — and this audit proves it."

How to use this guide: For each question, rate yourself honestly from 1–5. Add up your score per pillar, then use the Scoring Guide at the end to see your results.
